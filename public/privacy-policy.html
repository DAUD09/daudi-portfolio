Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Daudi Symon ("I", "me", "my") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit this portfolio website at daud09.github.io. I am committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about what limited data this site handles.

Information I Collect

This site collects minimal information, only when you choose to provide it:

  • Contact form submissions — when you use the "Get In Touch" form, your name, email address, subject, and message are submitted to and processed by Formspree (a third-party form service). I receive these details via email so I can respond to your enquiry.
  • No account registration — this site has no login, sign-up, or user accounts.
  • No automatic tracking — this site uses Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to collect anonymous traffic data such as page views, device type, and approximate location. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

Third-Party Services

This site loads resources from the following third-party services, which may set cookies or collect limited technical data (such as your IP address) as part of their normal operation:

  • Google Analytics 4 (GA4) — used to collect anonymous usage data including page views, session duration, device type, and traffic sources. This data helps me understand how visitors use the site. No personally identifiable information is collected. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts — used to load the Poppins typeface. Google may log font requests. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • jsDelivr CDN — used to load Bootstrap Icons and AOS animation library. See jsDelivr's Privacy Policy.
  • Formspree — processes contact form submissions. Your submitted data (name, email, message) is stored on Formspree's servers and forwarded to me. See Formspree's Privacy Policy.
  • GitHub Pages — this site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect basic server logs. See GitHub's Privacy Statement.

Cookies

This site does not set any first-party cookies. However, the third-party services listed above (Google Fonts, jsDelivr, Formspree, GitHub Pages) may set their own cookies or use similar browser storage technologies as part of their service delivery. I have no control over these third-party cookies. By using this site you acknowledge this limited third-party cookie activity.

Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained in my email inbox for as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry. Formspree retains submission data according to their own retention policy. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting me directly.
